# 📊 Logistics Performance & Profitability Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project is an end-to-end Power BI dashboard analysis of logistics operations, focusing on revenue performance, delivery efficiency, and profitability insights using DAX calculations and interactive dashboards.

The project is structured into three main dashboards: Overview, Operations, and Profitability.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## 📊 Dashboards Breakdown

### 1️⃣ Overview Dashboard
This dashboard provides a high-level view of business performance using key KPIs and revenue analysis.

**Key Metrics:**
- Total Revenue  
- Total Profit  
- Profit Margin %  
- On-time Performance  

**Visuals:**
- Total Revenue by Region  
- Revenue Trend Analysis  
- Top 10 Regions by Revenue  

---

### 2️⃣ Operations Dashboard
Focused on logistics efficiency and delivery performance across regions.

**Key Metrics:**
- On-time % by Region  
- Delay Analysis  
- Claims Analysis  

---

### 3️⃣ Profitability Dashboard
Analyzes profitability across service centers and cost structures.

**Key Metrics:**
- Top Profit Service Centers  
- Lowest Profit Service Centers  
- Profit by Service Center Name  
- Total Revenue vs Total Cost (by cost category)  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- North America contributes the highest share of total revenue, making it the key growth driver.  
- South Central shows the lowest delay rate, while Northeast requires improvement in delivery performance.  
- Houston, TX is the top-performing service center, while other centers show performance variation, indicating optimization opportunities.  

---

## 📈 Business Impact
This dashboard helps in:
- Monitoring logistics performance in real-time  
- Identifying high and low-performing regions  
- Improving delivery efficiency  
- Supporting data-driven decision making
